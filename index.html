About Deltape

Deltape Technologies Private Limited is an innovative online fee management platform designed to streamline and automate the fee collection process for educational institutes. Developed by a team of industry experts, Deltape leverages the latest technology to create a hassle-free experience for both administrators and parents. By digitalizing and automating the entire fee management lifecycle, Deltape empowers schools and universities to focus on their core mission of providing exceptional education, while ensuring timely and efficient fee collection.

Our Solutions

Delta zero

Zero-Interest Fee Installments

Delta Zero offers parents the flexibility to pay school fees in easy, zero-interest installments. This solution makes education more affordable by spreading out payments over time, relieving the financial burden of lump-sum fees. With Delta Zero, schools can offer a stress-free payment option that empowers parents to prioritize their children's education without worrying about large upfront costs.

Delta auto

Automated Fee Collection

Delta Auto ensures that schools receive fees on time without the hassle of follow-ups. This system automates fee collection through an auto-debit setup, allowing parents to set up payments and forget about them. Schools benefit from a steady cash flow and reduced administrative tasks, while parents enjoy the convenience of a seamless, automated payment process.

Delta pay

Low-Cost Payment Gateway

Delta Pay provides a simple, cost-effective way to handle all digital fee payments. This payment gateway easily integrates with your existing systems and supports all modes of digital payment. With Delta Pay, schools can offer a secure and efficient payment process, enhancing the experience for both parents and administrators while keeping transaction costs low.

EMI Options for Parents

Flexible Installments

Deltape's EMI (Equated Monthly Installment) feature allows parents to pay their child's fees in manageable monthly installments, making education more accessible and affordable.

Customized Plans

Deltape works closely with each institute to develop tailored EMI plans that cater to the diverse financial needs of their parent community, ensuring a seamless and inclusive fee payment experience.

Improved Cash Flow

By offering EMI options, Deltape helps educational institutes maintain a steady and predictable cash flow, enabling them to better plan and allocate their resources for the benefit of their students.

Integration with ERPs and CRMs

Seamless ERP Integration

Deltape seamlessly integrates with leading Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) systems, ensuring a smooth and efficient flow of student and fee-related data between the two platforms. This integration eliminates the need for manual data entry and reduces the risk of errors.

Powerful CRM Capabilities

Deltape's advanced Customer Relationship Management (CRM) features allow educational institutes to maintain a comprehensive database of student and parent information, facilitating better communication, personalized services, and targeted marketing efforts.

Seamless Data Exchange

The seamless integration between Deltape, ERPs, and CRMs creates a centralized and streamlined data ecosystem, enabling educational institutes to make informed decisions, improve operational efficiency, and enhance the overall student and parent experience.

How Deltape Works

  • Step 1
    Account Setup
    Deltape's onboarding process is designed to be quick and straightforward, with dedicated support to help educational institutes configure the platform according to their specific requirements.
  • Step 2
    Automated Debit Setup
    Deltape's intelligent payment engine seamlessly integrates with banks, allowing for the automatic deduction of fees from parents' accounts on predetermined schedules.
  • Step 3
    Reminders and Reporting
    Deltape's robust reporting and notification system keeps administrators and parents informed about payment status, outstanding dues, and other relevant fee-related information.
