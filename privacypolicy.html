Deltape Privacy Policy
Effective Date: 07/05/2024

Deltape ("we", "us", or "our") is a financial technology company ("FinTech") committed to protecting the privacy of our users ("you" or "your"). This Privacy Policy describes the types of information we collect from you or that you disclose to us when you use our website (https://deltape.in) (the "Website") and our mobile application (deltape) (collectively, the "Services") and how we may use that information.

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information when you use the Services:

  • Personal Information: This may include your name, email address, phone number, date of birth, government identification numbers (where required by law), and residential address.
  • Financial Information: This may include your bank account details, debit/credit card information, and transaction history (with your explicit consent).
  • Usage Data: This may include information about how you use the Services, such as the features you use, the time you spend on the Services, and the pages you visit.
  • Device Information: This may include information about the device you use to access the Services, such as your IP address, operating system, browser type, and device identifier.
How We Use Your Information

We may use the information we collect from you for the following purposes:

  • To provide and improve the Services, including processing fee payments and managing your financial accounts.
  • To personalize your experience with the Services.
  • To send you important account-related information and updates.
  • To comply with legal and regulatory requirements, including Know Your Customer (KYC) and Anti-Money Laundering (AML) regulations.
  • To detect and prevent fraud and other illegal activities.
  • To offer you targeted marketing communications (with your consent).
  • To analyze usage trends and improve the functionality of the Services.
Sharing Your Information

We may share your information with third-party service providers who help us operate the Services. These service providers are contractually obligated to protect your information and only use it for the purposes we have authorized. For example, we may share your information with:

  • Payment processors
  • Data security providers
  • Customer support providers

We will not sell or rent your personal information to third parties without your consent. However, we may disclose your information if we are required to do so by law or in the good faith belief that such disclosure is necessary to protect our rights or the rights of others.

Your Choices

You have choices regarding your information. You can:

  • Access and update your personal information through your account settings.
  • Opt out of receiving marketing communications by following the unsubscribe instructions in our emails or by contacting us.
  • Request that we delete your personal information (subject to certain legal and regulatory restrictions).
Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. This includes implementing industry-standard security measures such as encryption, firewalls, and access controls. However, no website or internet transmission is completely secure.

Children's Privacy

The Services are not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and you believe that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us. If we learn that we have collected personal information from a child under [insert age], we will delete that information.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on the Website and/or App. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@deltape.com

Additional Considerations for FinTech Companies

Here are some additional points to consider for your FinTech company's privacy policy:

  • Data Retention: You will need to specify how long you retain user data and under what circumstances.
  • Data Security Standards: Outline the specific security measures you take to protect financial information, such as complying with PCI DSS standards.
  • Regulatory Compliance: Ensure your policy complies with relevant data privacy regulations in your jurisdiction (e.g., GDPR, CCPA).
  • Dispute Resolution: Describe your process for resolving user concerns regarding their data privacy.
